I’m a doctoral candidate in philosophy at the University of North Carolina at Chapel Hill and an Affiliated Graduate Student in UNC’s PPE Program. My main areas of interest are political philosophy and PPE. I’m also interested in ethics (especially animal ethics and bioethics), ancient philosophy, and philosophy of religion.

I’m a dual citizen originally from São Paulo, Brazil. Before coming to UNC, I studied philosophy and religion at Harvard University. In my free time, I enjoy learning languages, playing the piano, powerlifting, and writing poetry.

I can be reached by email at jjporter@live.unc.edu.
