Languages


I love languages and have studied several different languages (ancient and modern) in both formal and informal settings.


I grew up in a bilingual household and learned both English and Portuguese at a young age. I studied Spanish (which is very similar to Portuguese) throughout high school and then Attic and Koine Greek, Classical Hebrew, and Latin at the undergraduate and graduate levels. I have also been self-teaching German (slowly but surely!) for the better part of a decade, as well as French and Italian. (I also dabble in Irish, Scottish Gaelic, and Welsh.)
