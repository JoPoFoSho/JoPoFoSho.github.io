I’m involved in a number of the Prindle Institute’s outreach efforts:


  • Courageous Conversations in Ethics, a series of moderated conversations on contentious ethical topics (in which I have participated as a co-discussant
  • Edible Ethics, a series of ethics discussion tables in DePauw’s dining hall
  • Fireside Chats, a series of weekly ethics discussions for residents of a local nursing home
  • Prindlepalooza, an annual event introducing hundreds of DePauw students to Prindle’s ethics initiatives
    • ul>

    I also fulfill a range of other administrative responsibilities at Prindle, including coordinating the upcoming Midwest Ethics Symposium, mentoring undergraduate interns, refereeing ethics reading course proposals, and strategic planning.


    Beyond Prindle and DePauw, I’m active in the PPE Society, for which I’ve served as a conference co-organizer, referee, session chair, and presenter.
