Through my work at the Prindle Institute for Ethics, I’m involved in a range of ethics outreach efforts:


  • Courageous Conversations in Ethics, a series of moderated conversations on difficult ethical topics (in which I’ve participated as a co-discussant)
  • Edible Ethics, a series of ethics discussion tables in DePauw’s dining hall
  • Prindlepalooza, an event introducing hundreds of DePauw students each year to Prindle’s ethics initiatives
  • Indiana High School Ethics Bowl
  • And much more

I also hold a number of other administrative responsibilities at Prindle, including coordinating our upcoming Midwest Ethics Symposium, mentoring undergraduate interns, refereeing ethics reading course proposals, and contributing to long-term strategic planning.


Beyond Prindle and DePauw, I’m active in the PPE Society, for which I’ve served as a conference co-organizer, referee, session chair, and presenter.
